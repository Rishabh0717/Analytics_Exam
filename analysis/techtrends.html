TechTrends Analytics Report

1. Executive Summary

This report presents key findings from an in-depth analysis of TechTrends' customer data, encompassing both descriptive and predictive analytics. Our analysis reveals significant insights into customer behavior, purchasing patterns, and the effectiveness of promotional strategies.

2. Key Insights from Exploratory Data Analysis

2.1 Customer Demographics and Behavior

  • The average customer age is 30 years, with incomes ranging from $5,000 to $49,000 annually.
  • The customer base is fairly evenly split between male and female customers, with a slight majority being male.
  • Customers with higher education levels (Masters and Bachelors) tend to have higher average purchase amounts.

2.2 Purchase Patterns and Product Categories

  • Electronics and Clothing categories show the highest median purchase amounts and the widest range of values.
  • The East and North regions have the highest number of customers across all purchase frequency categories.
  • Gold status customers demonstrate higher purchase amounts across all purchase frequency levels and report the highest satisfaction scores.

2.3 Promotional Effectiveness

  • Approximately 24.5% of customers used promotional offers.
  • Electronics and Clothing categories have the highest proportion of promotion usage.
  • Customers who used promotions have a higher average purchase amount ($10,861) compared to those who didn't ($9,853).
