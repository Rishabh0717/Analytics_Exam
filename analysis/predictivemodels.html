2. Promotional Offer Usage Prediction Model

Model Performance:

  • Accuracy: 78%
  • Precision: 71%
  • Recall: 68%
  • F1-score: 69%

Key Predictors:

  1. Income (Importance: 0.1724)
  2. Purchase Amount (Importance: 0.1406)
  3. Age (Importance: 0.1050)

Implications: This model indicates that income, purchase amount, and age are the most important factors in predicting whether a customer will use promotional offers. The model's performance suggests it's a useful tool for targeting promotions, but there's room for improvement.

3. Overall Implications for TechTrends

These models provide valuable insights for TechTrends:

  • Income-based segmentation should be a key strategy in both product offerings and promotional targeting.
  • Age-specific marketing and product recommendations can improve both purchase amounts and promotional effectiveness.
  • While satisfaction scores impact purchase amounts, they're less crucial for promotional strategies, suggesting different approaches may be needed for increasing sales versus promoting offers.
  • The strong link between purchase amounts and promotional usage suggests that targeting big spenders with promotions could be particularly effective.

By leveraging these insights, TechTrends can optimize its marketing strategies, improve customer targeting, and ultimately drive sales growth.
